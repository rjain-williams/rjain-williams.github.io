Designing for others

Below is the introduction to this design sprint, which will contextualize it for you.

"For this design sprint, we were tasked with redesigning a rather primitive online book catalog website. We began our design process by examining the existing book catalog and all of its features. We started our ideation process by each examining the current book catalog and jotting down a few re-design ideas we think would be beneficial for the usability of the catalog, individually. We then came together and shared our ideas, discussing how they could work together, feasibility, usability, and lastly, how we would implement them."
