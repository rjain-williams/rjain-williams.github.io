Designing for others

Below is the introduction to this design sprint, which will contextualize it for you.

"During our human-computer interaction class, we were challenged to design a mobile interface that improves upon our school’s Global Education & Study Away website, with a specific focus on graduating seniors. Our goal was to create a more intuitive, visually appealing, and user-friendly experience that caters to seniors' needs as they plan their postgraduate study away opportunities. Our team, composed of Tao Chen, Milton Vento, Rijul Jain, and Skylar Yarter, spent the past week analyzing our school’s website (link here), creating mood boards, conducting user research, and iterating on designs using Figma. We documented our entire process and rationale, which we present below."
